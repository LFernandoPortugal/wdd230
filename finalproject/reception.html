Accomodations

Food service and setup

  • Aperitif Catering

    This service lasts between 15 to 30 minutes. It is made based on a calculation of 2 bites per person.

  • Brunch Catering

    It is the combination of breakfast and lunch, its estimated service time is at 11 am and its approximate duration is 1 hour and a half.

  • Catering Reception

    It lasts approximately 3 hours and is often done at 8:00 p.m., 2/3 are salty and 1/3 sweet.

  • Catering Buffet Reception

    Like the previous one, its duration is 3 hours and the estimated time is at 8:00 p.m. But in this case, varieties of hot and cold dishes and side dishes are offered.

Traditions and Photography

You may have seen people throwing rice, honking horns, or decorating vehicles at weddings. None of that is appropriate on temple grounds.
Obviously, photography is not allowed inside the temple, but wedding photos can be taken anywhere else on the temple grounds.
